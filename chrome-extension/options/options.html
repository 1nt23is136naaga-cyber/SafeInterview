ℹ️ About

AntiGravity analyses interview integrity by combining speech pattern analysis, semantic similarity detection, and behavioural monitoring. All analysis is performed by your backend — this extension is the capture and display layer.

⚖️ Ethics: Analysis focuses on behavioural patterns, not accent, language, or demographics. Candidate consent must be obtained before use.
